2012 National Juried Group Exhibition

 

January 2012

NKG is pleased to present a varied exhibition of artists from all parts of the USA. Please join us for this interesting and rich experience. Many different approaches and ideas are presented, mirroring the complexity of the 21st Century. Come and see a slice of the art world, the tip of the iceberg, as we enter a new year.

We will be choosing one artist who has work in this exhibition to receive a solo exhibition in 2012.

Images of the work will be posted on January 5th, 2012
