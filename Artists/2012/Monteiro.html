John Monteiro

 

March 2012

NKG is pleased to present the masterful and whimsical drawings of Bostonian artist John Monteiro. John Monteiro is a master of the line and form.  His images cannot be appreciated when seen solely via digital means, and a reproduction of his work cannot capture the beauty of the lines he so meticulously creates with his hand.   His work embodies an intelligence and conceptual framing of our world that goes beyond the ordinary. NKG is pleased to introduce John Monteiro’s work.  Please come and enjoy his amazing drawings.
