Temporal Identities


May 2010

NKG presented a May 2010 exhibition titled Temporal Identities. This show included the works of four active Massachusetts artists: Cathy McLaurin, Louise Weinberg, Helga Felleisen, and Kelly Anona Kerrigan. Their work conceptually examines the hidden, inconstant or nonconcrete nature of identity. With elements of painting, drawing and installation in play, these artists challenge the temporal quality of sense of self. This exhibition was available May 5 - 28, 2010 with opening reception May 7, 5 - 8 PM
