ArtSpeak!


August 2011

NKG invited artists to participate in this conceptual performance exhibition of the artist statement in our contemporary art world. Come one Come All! Send us statements via e-mail or stop by our gallery and drop off your artist statements and we will create an entire environment to participate in this important discourse aimed at transparency in the art world. Performance artists, sound artists are also welcome to participate in ways beyond the submission of statements for the opening reception and party on First Friday August 5 2011. The exhibition will be open to the public on Fridays and Saturdays until August 26, 2011
