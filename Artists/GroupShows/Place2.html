Place


June 2011

NKG presents a June 2011 exhibition titled PLACE. This show includes the works of four active Massachusetts artists: Prilla Smith-Brackett, , Shay Culligan, Ruth Feldman, and Dorothea VanCamp. Their work conceptually examines the meaning of the tangible, inconstant or nonconcrete nature of Place. With elements of painting, drawing, and printmaking in play, these artists explore the qualities that the word Place can embody, from the metaphorical, to the concrete, you will not be disappointed by experiencing their visions. This exhibition is available June 3 - 26, 2011 with opening reception June 3, 5 - 8 PM.
